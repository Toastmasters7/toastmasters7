About Us

Why Join This Club?

San Diego Toastmasters 7 has been a cornerstone of leadership and communication development in San Diego for over 85 years. Our club provides a supportive environment where members can practice public speaking, develop leadership skills, and build lasting friendships.

Whether you're looking to overcome a fear of public speaking, advance your career, or simply become a more confident communicator, our club offers the resources and support you need to succeed.

Club Awards

Throughout our distinguished history, San Diego Toastmasters 7 has earned numerous awards and recognitions for excellence in club performance, member development, and community impact.

Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our consistent achievement of Distinguished Club status and the individual accomplishments of our members.
