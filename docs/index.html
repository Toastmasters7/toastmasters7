How Does It Work?

The environment in a Toastmasters club is friendly and supportive. Everyone at a Toastmasters meeting feels welcome and valued ‚Äì from beginners to advanced speakers.

In a club meeting, you practice giving prepared speeches as well as brief impromptu presentations, known as Table Topics. There is no rush or pressure to get on stage: The Toastmasters program allows you to progress at your own pace.

Constructive evaluation is central to the Toastmasters philosophy. Each time you give a prepared speech, an evaluator will point out strengths as well as suggest improvements. Receiving ‚Äì and giving ‚Äì such feedback is a great learning experience. In Toastmasters, encouragement and improvement go hand-in-hand.
